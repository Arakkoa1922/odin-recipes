Margarita

Description

A good drink for coding while digesting all that food you ate at the Mexican place.

The Mexican place in my small town makes ridiculously strong versions of this!

Ingredients

  • Lime juice
  • Tequila
  • Triple sec

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients with ice in a shaker.
  2. Shake and pour into hurricane glass.
  3. That's really it, margaritas are simple.
