White Russian

Description

A good drink for coding while accompanied by your Blue Russian cat.

Did you know that I made one of these to drink while making this project?

Ingredients

  • 5 parts vodka
  • 3 parts cream
  • 2 parts coffee liqueur

Steps

  1. Fill glass with ice.
  2. Pour vodka and coffee liqueur over ice.
  3. Float cream on top.
  4. Stir slowly.
