Blue Hawaiian

Description

A good drink for coding while pretending you're not in freaking Missouri.

Did you know that I really like the color on these?

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Sweet and Sour
  • 3/4 oz Light Rum
  • 1/2 oz Curacao
  • 3/4 oz Vodka
  • 3 oz pineapple juice
  • Simple syrup

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients with ice.
  2. Stir and pour into hurricane glass.
  3. Garnish with a cocktail umbrella and cherry.
